Kitchen Communication & Safety

Essential Callouts

CalloutMeaningUse When
"Behind!"Someone is passing behind youAny time walking behind a teammate in tight quarters
"Hot!"Carrying something very hotMoving hot pans, pots, or dishes through the kitchen
"Corner!"Turning a blind cornerPrevents collisions at intersections
"86!"Item is out of stockAlert expo and FOH managers immediately

Knife Safety Rules

  • Carry knives point-down, blade away from your body
  • Never leave a knife in a sink — someone could reach in and cut themselves
  • Announce "sharp" when handing a knife to someone
  • Always use a cut-resistant glove on the non-dominant hand when applicable

Food Safety & Sanitation

Temperature Control

CategoryTemperature
Danger Zone (bacteria multiply rapidly)41°F – 135°F
Cold holding41°F or below
Hot holding135°F or above
Poultry (chicken)165°F internal
Ground beef155°F internal
Fish and seafood145°F internal
Pork145°F internal

FIFO — First In, First Out

  • New product goes to the back; older product stays in front
  • Label everything with prep date and use-by date
  • If it's past its date or smells off — alert a manager, do not use it

Cooler Storage Order (top to bottom)

  1. Ready-to-eat foods (top)
  2. Seafood
  3. Whole cuts of beef and pork
  4. Ground meat
  5. Poultry (bottom)

Handwashing (20 seconds minimum)

Always wash hands after: handling raw protein, using restroom, taking out trash, touching face/hair, returning from break, handling money.
